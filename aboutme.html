About Me

Thanks for visiting my page! I'm a recent graduate of the University of Virginia hoping to break into the field of Data Analytics. While in school, I studied Economics and Data Analytics, which helped me discover my passion for analytics as a field. I'm using this site a a way to showcase my ever-developing skillset and my constant curiousity for the world around me.

If you see anything that may be of interest anywhere on my page, don't hesitate to reach out! My contact information can be found in the footer of any page. I'll do my best to reply as fast as humanely possible.
