What Makes Modern Music Popular?

This was the final project for a course in Linear Regression I took while at the University of Virginia. After coming across a data set containing information of over 160,000 songs with release dates ranging from the 1920s to 2020. Exploratory data analysis was completed using SAS and a three step analysis was completed before arriving at a final regression model.

To save you a click, the most important factors to determine a song's popularity were: danceability, energy, and valence. With that being said. I would highly recommend checking out the rest of the paper to read about concerns and future considerations.
